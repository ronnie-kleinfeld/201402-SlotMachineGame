Interfaces
 InterfaceDescription
 Counter The Counter interface must be implemented by all counters.
Classes
 ClassDescription
 Blast The Blast counter causes the emitter to create a single burst of particles when it starts and then emit no further particles.
 KeyDownCounter The KeyDownCounter Counter modifies another counter to only emit particles when a specific key is being pressed.
 PerformanceAdjusted The PerformanceAdjusted counter causes the emitter to emit particles continuously at a steady rate.
 Pulse The Pulse counter causes the emitter to emit groups of particles at a regular interval.
 Random The Random counter causes the emitter to emit particles continuously at a variable random rate between two limits.
 SineCounter The Sine counter causes the emitter to emit particles continuously at a rate that varies according to a sine wave.
 Steady The Steady counter causes the emitter to emit particles continuously at a steady rate.
 TimePeriod The TimePeriod counter causes the emitter to emit particles for a period of time and then stop.
 ZeroCounter The Zero counter causes the emitter to emit no particles.
